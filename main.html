Student Behavior Standards

All students must follow these rules:

Virus Prevention

  • Wear a mask when feeling unwell
  • Sneeze into your elbow
  • Bring your own supplies

Greetings

  • Smile and greet everyone
  • Greet your teachers

Communication

  • Use proper English
  • Respect the person speaking
  • Ask permission before questions

Responsibility

  • Arrive early to school
  • Wear uniform properly
  • Keep yourself and school clean
  • Take care of your things
  • Submit assignments on time
  • Wait in line properly
  • Behave during school events

Permissions

  • Use hall pass for restroom
  • Fill forms when leaving class
  • Fill forms when leaving school

Examinations

  • Take all exams on time
  • Follow exam rules
  • Valid reasons needed for missing exams

Worship Time

  • Pay attention during worship
  • Take notes during sermons
  • No talking or homework during worship
  • Don't disturb others
  • Bring your Bible
